## Build phase (2026-06-21) — Story 1.5/1.6

- **DECISION (Operator) — LeRobot adoption boundary (resolves Open Q2):** `arm.py`'s driver is re-based onto LeRobot's `lerobot.motors.feetech.FeetechMotorsBus` (which wraps `scservo_sdk`), exchanging RAW encoder steps (`normalize=False`) so Dum-E's centered-degrees math + soft limits need no LeRobot calibration dict. The FR-14 safety chokepoint is unchanged: the LeRobot bus is encapsulated in `arm._FeetechBus` (raw-steps surface: `read_steps`/`setup`/`write_goal`/`engage`/`release`), and a test guard forbids both the LeRobot Feetech bus and raw `scservo_sdk` outside `arm.py`. Added `arm.hold()/relax()` (per-joint torque on/off) to support hand-sweep range calibration. Servo joint-range calibration (FR-11) leans on LeRobot's calibration facility (homing offsets + range-of-motion).
- **NON-ADOPTION (Operator) — defer `SOFollower` to v2:** considered adopting LeRobot's SO-101 follower *robot* class as the arm backend; rejected for v1. It bundles camera ownership (conflicts with the tuned `camera.py` sole-owner/anti-flicker path), provides only a coarse `max_relative_target` motion clamp (weaker than the FR-14 chokepoint, which would still have to wrap it), and assumes a gripper on motor 6 (ours is a camera-pan half-leader). Its real value — normalized observation/action spaces for `LeRobotDataset` recording + policy rollout — is a v2 concern; adopt it then.
- **STATUS — servos 2-6 range/wrap calibration still pending on hardware:** soft limits in `config.yaml` remain placeholder `[-180,180]`; the `sweep`/`analyze` + LeRobot-calibration path is now unblocked (hold/relax landed) but not yet run on the bench. Driver re-base needs a hardware smoke-test (can't be verified off-bench).

## Bench validation (2026-06-21) — Story 1.6 driver re-base + range calibration

- **VALIDATED ON HARDWARE:** LeRobot driver re-base proven on the real arm — read path (bringup: 6 joints + camera), torque hold/relax (register-confirmed on/off, `hold`=0-step jump), and accurate velocity-capped motion (+3°→+32 steps, clean net-0 return). The risky part (swapping the driver under FR-14) works.
- **Range calibration validated on joint 4:** LeRobot `set_half_turn_homings` re-zeroed joint 4 from +158° (3846 steps, hugging the wrap) to true-centre; measured travel 110.8°; soft limit `[-52.4, 52.4]`, 96° clear of the wrap. Persisted to `calibration/joints.json` (gitignored) + homing offset in servo EEPROM.
- **DESIGN CHANGE — `calibrate.py range` is now a non-interactive powered-recenter flow (one joint per run):** hold whole arm → free target → rough re-zero (wrap far away) → timed hand-sweep to measure range → **power the joint to the measured midpoint and re-zero there (true centre)** → symmetric soft limit. Replaces the original interactive LeRobot `record_ranges_of_motion` flow, which needed a TTY (ENTER/live-table) and left the arm sagging (relaxed all). The powered re-center gives a precise symmetric range without hand-parking. Operator's idea — measure-then-center beats eyeballing.
- **HARDENING:** `arm.read_steps` now retries (num_retry=2) — a single dropped Feetech status packet under motion was aborting captures/moves. Persistence (`calibration/joints.json`, v2) accumulates one joint at a time so joints can be calibrated separately.
